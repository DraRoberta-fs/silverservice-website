About Silver Service

Quality You Can Trust

At Silver Service Takeaway, we serve authentic pizzas, kebabs, and burgers made with the finest fresh ingredients in Ipswich.

Our pizzas are specially prepared with mozzarella and cheddar cheese on thin crust and baked fresh on the premises. Our kebabs feature fresh lamb and chicken, marinated and grilled to perfection. Located on Ulster Avenue, we offer both delivery and collection.
